I am a Product Leader with deep experience across financial services, compliance, and consumer experience. At Goldman Sachs, I led new application flows and existing member experience for the business credit card at Marcus. I then moved into Compliance Product Management, building internal tools that compliance teams would actually want to use while keeping auditors happy.

Before Goldman Sachs, I helped scale an agri-startup in India and built sales analytics platforms across the US and India. I hold an MBA with a focus on Technical Product Management from the University of Texas at Austin and a B.Tech from IIT Delhi.

Now I'm a mum to a wonderful little girl, and I'm using this chapter to do the thing I've always enjoyed: build stuff. I'm getting hands-on with AI, experimenting with vibe coding, and making things I wish existed — and hoping others want them too. So far, the user research sample size is one 😉 — but I'm already plotting how to find my people and share what I build along the way 💪

If you're curious about my work, want to collaborate, or just want to talk product — I'd love to hear from you.
